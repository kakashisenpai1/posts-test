# Pancake Recipe

Here's a delicious pancake recipe:

**Ingredients:**

- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup milk
- 1 egg
- 2 tablespoons melted butter

**Instructions:**

1. In a bowl, mix the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
2. In another bowl, whisk the milk, egg, and melted butter.
3. Pour the wet mixture into the dry mixture while gently stirring until combined.
4. Heat a griddle and pour pancake batter onto it. Cook until bubbles form on the surface.
5. Flip the pancake and cook until both sides are golden brown.
6. Serve warm with maple syrup and fruit slices.

Enjoy your delicious breakfast!
